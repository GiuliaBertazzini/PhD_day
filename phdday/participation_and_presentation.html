Participation and presentation instructions



How to apply

PhD students that are interested in giving a talk should register using the following google form. The deadline for the application is May 12th. Slides for the the presentation must be uploaded before May 21st to the link that will be given once registered.

For poster presentation PhD students should register using the following google form. The deadline for the application is May 5th. Posters must be uploaded in .pdf format before May 12th to the link that will be given once registered.

A maximum number of 12 presenters will be allowed for each department at the Engineering School, belonging to XXXVII, XXXVII or the XXXIX cycles. In case the number of applications will exceed the maximum, the 12 presenters will be randomly selected in each department. In case the maximum number of presenters for a department will not be reached, the time slots will be allocated to the other departments.

Ted talks

Presentations will be given in English. 3 minutes will be allocated to each talk. Templates for presentation are available here (To be done).

Posters

All PhD students can exhibit a poster. The poster should be prepared in A2 format and should include the date, logo, sponsors and QR codes. A poster template is available here (To be done). The available poster templates are just examples, it is possible to customize your own.
The only requirements are:
– A2 format
– the poster should include the QR code of your PhD program web site
– the poster should include the QR code of your on line poster (we will communicate you the web address after the deadline for poster registration). From there it will be possible to vote for your poster.
