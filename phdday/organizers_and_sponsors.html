Organizers and supporting companies

The PhD Day is jointly organized by the four doctorate programs at the School of Engineering, University of Florence:
– International Doctorate in Civil and Environmental Engineering, at the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering (DICEA)
– PhD in Industrial Engineering at the Department of Industrial Engineering (DIEF)
– PhD in Information Engineering at the Department of Information Engineering (DINFO)
– PhD in Smart Computing at the Department of Information Engineering (DINFO)
